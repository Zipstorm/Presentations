## 4. Platform-Specific Research Moves

### YouTube

**Why primary**: Highest trust, longest content engagement, tutorial-friendly format. Indian YouTube is massive — 462M+ users.

**Search queries to run now:**

- `resume tips India` → sort by view count (last year)
- `how to get a job in India 2026`
- `career advice for freshers`
- `placement preparation tips`
- `LinkedIn profile tips India`
- `AI tools for job search`
- `resume kaise banaye` (Hindi)
- `portfolio building tips`

**What to look for:**

- ✅ Videos with 50K+ views on career/resume topics
- ✅ Comment sections full of questions (= engaged job-seeking audience)
- ✅ Creator does product integrations/sponsorships (check descriptions for "#ad" or "sponsored")
- ✅ Consistent upload schedule (weekly+)
- 🚩 Subscriber count growing but views declining = disengaged audience
- 🚩 Comment section full of bots or generic praise
- 🚩 No sponsored content history = might be hard to partner with

**5 starting accounts to explore:**

1. **Ankur Warikoo** (@warikoo) — 4M+ subs. Career/entrepreneurship. Does sponsorships. Highest-reach option.
2. **Aman Dhattarwal** — 3M+ subs. Student/fresher audience. Placement-focused.
3. **Ishan Sharma** — 1.5M+. Tech/startup career content. Young audience.
4. **Akshay Saini** (NamasteDev) — 1.7M subs. Developer careers. Technical audience.
5. **Meher Sindhu Batra** — Growing. Pure career coaching. Resume tips, interview prep. Most niche-relevant.

### Reference Case Studies

**Meesho — Micro-Influencer Blitz**

- 67,000 app downloads in 30 days using micro-influencers
- Targeted Tier 2 & 3 cities with regional content strategy
- Separate campaign: 30M+ reach with under 20 creators in 2 weeks (#MeeshoPeHaiSabKuch)
- Lesson: Micro-influencers + regional targeting = high conversion at low cost

**CRED — Influencer-Led Launch**

- 50% increase in app downloads attributed to influencer marketing
- Used humor-driven content with celebrity + micro-influencer mix
- Lesson: Creative, shareable content format matters more than follower count

**Apna App — YouTube Integration**

- Used YouTube integrated content (not standalone ads) to drive app installs
- Strategy: partnered with career/education creators for organic-feeling tutorials
- Lesson: "How to use" content converts better than "check out this app" content
