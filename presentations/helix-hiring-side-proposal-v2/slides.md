**Date**: 2026-04-06

## Slide 1: Title
Persona Reframing — Helix Hiring Side Proposal V2

---

## Slide 2: Core Idea — Two Surfaces, No Persona Switcher
- Replace persona switcher with simple side nav: Hiring / Applying
- Two surfaces, one product

---

## Slide 3: Onboarding Option A — Step 1
- Two-step selection: surface choice first
- "I'm Hiring" vs "I'm Looking for a Job"

---

## Slide 4: Onboarding Option A — Step 2
- Hiring users pick a role: Hiring Manager or Recruiter
- Can always change later in Settings

---

## Slide 5: Onboarding Option B — Nested Cards
- One screen with Hiring card containing two sub-options
- Applying is a standalone card

---

## Slide 6: Hiring Manager Experience
- "My Jobs" — scoped to jobs the HM owns
- Jobs created via job wizard + Sam voice conversation
- Actions: share, review candidates, invite collaborators

---

## Slide 7: Recruiter Experience
- "Jobs" — org-wide scope (all connected ATS jobs)
- Jobs imported from ATS
- Actions: run AI evaluation, curate shortlists, send to HM for review

---

## Slide 8: Settings — Role Switcher
- Hiring Manager: see and manage jobs you create
- Recruiter: access ATS-imported jobs across your org

---

## Slide 9: Summary
- Navigation: Two surfaces in side nav, no persona switcher
- Onboarding: Surface first, then role. Two options: two-step vs nested cards
- Hiring Manager: Own jobs, create via wizard, share & collaborate
- Recruiter: Org-wide, ATS import, AI evaluation, curate shortlists
